Privacy Policy

Teleprompter for Android — Effective date: 25 May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Teleprompter application for Android (package: com.vlad.teleprompter), a free app developed by Vladislav Popov.

1. Introduction

Teleprompter ("the App") is a free Android application that displays scrolling text on your device screen to assist with presentations, speeches, and video recording. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains what information the App does and does not collect.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.) is collected.
  • No usage analytics or tracking is implemented.
  • No user accounts or login are required.
  • No data is transmitted to any external server.
  • The App does not require an internet connection to function.
  • All text content you enter into the teleprompter remains on your device only.

3. App Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions to provide its core functionality:

  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW (Display over other apps): Required to display the teleprompter text as an overlay on top of other applications, including your camera app. This is a core feature of the App and no data is collected through this permission.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required to keep the teleprompter overlay running reliably while you use other applications. This ensures the scrolling text remains visible during recording or presentations.
  • RECORD_AUDIO (Microphone): This permission exists in the App's codebase for potential future functionality. It is not actively used in the current version. If and when this feature is enabled, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly. No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Future advertising: We may introduce advertisements through Google AdMob in a future version of the App. If and when this occurs:
  • Google AdMob may collect certain data such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • You will be able to opt out of personalised advertising through your device's advertising settings.
  • This privacy policy will be updated before any advertising is introduced.
  • For more information on how Google uses data, visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

5. Data Storage

All data created within the App (such as teleprompter scripts and settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be removed from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since the App does not collect any personal data from any user, no special provisions for children are required. The App is suitable for general audiences.

7. Data Security

Since the App does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers, the risk of data breach is minimal. All user-created content remains on the device and is subject to the security measures of your Android operating system.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR and UK Data Protection Law

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have certain rights regarding your personal data. Since the App does not collect any personal data, these rights are not directly applicable. However, should this change in the future (for example, through the introduction of advertising services), you will have the right to:

  • Access any personal data held about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Data portability.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Significant changes (such as the introduction of data collection through advertising) will be communicated through the App's store listing or an in-app notice.
